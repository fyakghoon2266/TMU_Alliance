National Cheng Kung University

National Cheng Kung University

Currently, there are no partner universities in Africa. However, a significant number of medical and healthcare professionals within the university have international collaboration experience, and the affiliated hospitals also have extensive experience in overseas medical missions. Through this alliance cooperation, it is possible to establish a network of collaboration in Africa, which will facilitate the recruitment of students from African countries to study at the university and expand the influence of the university and its affiliated institutions in Africa.
